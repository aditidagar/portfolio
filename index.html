Who I am

Software Developer based out of Toronto

I'm a fourth year student at University of Toronto studying Computer Science and Mathematics.

Currently, I'm working at the Continuum Robotics Laboratory as a summer research student. My project involves implementing models of a tendon-driven continuum robot to be used as a catheter for contact-aided navigation.

I have a keen interest in Software Development, Data Science and Math Education. I also work as an undergraduate Teaching Assistant and have led tutorials for introductory courses in Calculus and Discrete Mathematics.
